How do we define Consciousness?

APA

  1. state of being conscious.
  2. an organism's awarenss of something either internal or external to itself.
  3. the waking state (wakefulness)
  4. in medicine and brain science, the distinctive electrical activity of the waking brain, as recorded via scalp electroencephalogram, that is commonly used to identify conscious states and their pathologies.

David M. Armstrong

Sentience: a creature capable of sensing and responding to its world.

Oxford Languages Definition

the state of being awake and aware of one's surroundings; the fact of awareness by the mind of itself and the world

Merriam-Webster Dictionary

the quality or state of being aware especially of something within oneself; the state of being characterized by sensation, emotion, volition, and thought

Thomas Nagel

something that it is like to be [a particular thing/organism]

Michael S. A. Graziano

the act of being conscious of something, rather than the material of which you are conscious
